Curious about me? Here you have it:

I'm a passionate self-proclaimed designer who specializes in full stack development (React.js & Node.js). I love bringing technical and visual aspects of digital products to life. User experience, pixel-perfect design, and writing clear, readable, and highly performant code matters to me.

I began my journey as a web developer in 2015, and since then, I've evolved by tackling new challenges and learning the latest technologies. Now, in my early thirties, I'm building cutting-edge web applications using modern stacks like Next.js, TypeScript, NestJS, TailwindCSS, Supabase, and more.

I’m a progressive thinker who enjoys working on products from ideation all the way to development.

When I'm not coding, I hang around Twitter or Indie Hackers, witnessing the journey of startups or enjoying some free time. You can follow me on Twitter or GitHub.
